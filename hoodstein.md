# HoodStein

## Real Name
Unknown

## Role
Primary Hero of the HoodStein Universe

## Origin
HoodStein was created during a secret storm-energy experiment led by scientist Michael Volt.  
The experiment unleashed a massive electrical storm that fused lightning energy with a mysterious body recovered from the streets.  

The result was HoodStein — a powerful being capable of channeling electricity and storm energy.

## Abilities
- Lightning manipulation
- Superhuman strength
- Electrical regeneration
- Storm sensing

## Allies
- The Monster Pack
- Plug (robotic pet companion)

## Enemies
- Michael Volt
